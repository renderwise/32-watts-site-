The finished aligners pass through stringent in-house quality check for the safety and smooth margins. The material used is FDA and CE approved.

BIO MECHANICAL PRINCIPLE

Applying light force is the key to optimum tooth movement. We use the optimum thickness of aligners, to control the force levels with the help of attachments. This is a technique used to make the aligners more effective and biomechanically sound so that we achieve the desired precise and predictable results.
